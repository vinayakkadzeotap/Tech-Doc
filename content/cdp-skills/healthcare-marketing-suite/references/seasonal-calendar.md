# Healthcare Seasonal Calendar

Strategic calendar for healthcare, pharmaceutical, wellness, and health insurance marketing campaigns aligned with clinical windows, enrollment periods, and patient engagement patterns.

## Q1 (January - March)

### January
- **New Year Resolution Peak**: Fitness goals, weight loss, wellness program enrollment surge
- **Winter Flu Season Peak**: Influenza vaccination outreach, cold/flu treatment messaging
- **New Insurance Plans Active**: Coverage begins, deductible resets, new plan education
- **Back-to-School Health Prep**: Children immunizations (RSV, flu catch-up), school physicals
- **Mental Health Awareness**: Seasonal affective disorder (SAD) resources, mental health support

**Campaign Focus**: Wellness enrollments, flu vaccination, mental health resources, deductible education

### February
- **Heart Health Month**: Cardiovascular disease awareness, heart disease risk screening
- **Black History Month**: Health equity messaging, health disparities resources
- **Presidents Day Weekend**: Telehealth and quick-visit messaging for minor illnesses
- **Tax Season Stress**: Mental health and stress management resources
- **Flu Season Decline**: Flu vaccination completion messaging, antiviral treatment info

**Campaign Focus**: Cardiovascular risk screening, heart disease prevention, mental health support

### March
- **Colorectal Cancer Awareness Month**: Screening recommendations, prevention education
- **Daylight Saving Time**: Sleep hygiene and health routine resources
- **Asthma and Allergy Awareness Month**: Allergy triggers, asthma management, inhaler usage
- **Spring Break Health Prep**: Travelers' health, vaccination requirements, emergency care resources
- **Mental Health Month (begins late March)**: Depression, anxiety awareness, treatment access

**Campaign Focus**: Cancer screening, allergy management, sleep health, travel health

### April

- **Autism Awareness Month**: Family resources, developmental screening, early intervention
- **Stress Awareness Month**: Workplace stress, mindfulness, mental health resources
- **Tax Deadline Stress**: Financial strain health impact, stress management resources
- **Parkinson's Awareness Month**: Early signs, movement disorder resources, treatment options

**Campaign Focus**: Autism screening, stress management, environmental health, Parkinson's awareness

### May
- **Mental Health Awareness Month**: Depression, anxiety, substance use disorder messaging
- **Lupus Awareness Month**: Autoimmune disease education, specialist referrals
- **Maternal Health Month**: Prenatal care, pregnancy resources, postpartum support
- **Memorial Day Weekend**: Veteran mental health, PTSD resources, healthcare access
- **Bladder Cancer Awareness Month**: Hematuria, cancer screening education

**Campaign Focus**: Mental health support, maternal health, veteran health resources

### June
- **Men's Health Month**: Prostate cancer, cardiovascular disease, mental health in men
- **Pride Month (LGBTQ+ Health)**: LGBTQ+-affirming care resources, preventive health equity
- **World Blood Donor Day**: Blood donation opportunities, donation eligibility education
- **Alzheimer's & Brain Awareness Month**: Cognitive decline resources, family support
- **Summer Vacation Health Prep**: Travel vaccines, medication supplies, telehealth info

**Campaign Focus**: Men's health screening, LGBTQ+ health equity, Alzheimer's awareness, travel medicine

## Cross-Seasonal Patterns

### Open Enrollment Windows
- **Medicare Enrollment**: October 15 - December 7 (annual)
- **Individual/Marketplace Enrollment**: November 1 - January 15 (varies by state)
- **Employer Plan Enrollment**: October-November typically (varies by employer)
- Begin outreach 4-6 weeks before enrollment periods with education and support

### Clinical Windows & Screening Recommendations
- **Annual Preventive Care**: Best Jan-Feb after deductible resets and holidays complete
- **Cancer Screenings**: Align messaging with recommended screening ages and frequencies
- **Immunization Windows**: Age-based and risk-based vaccination schedules throughout year
- **Chronic Disease Management**: Quarterly check-ins recommended, not seasonal dependent

### Health Awareness Months
- **January**: Mental health (New Year focus)
- **February**: Heart health, Black history
- **March**: Colorectal cancer, asthma/allergy
- **April**: Autism, stress awareness
- **May**: Mental health, maternal health, lupus
- **June**: Men's health, LGBTQ+ health, Alzheimer's
- **July-August**: Diverse awareness months (refer to specific calendar dates)
- **September**: Suicide prevention, childhood cancer
- **October**: Breast cancer, mental health, flu vaccination kickoff
- **November**: Diabetes, lung cancer, Thanksgiving wellness
- **December**: Year-end wellness, New Year resolutions, flu season peak

### Engagement Pattern Drivers
- **Weather**: Cold season (Nov-Feb) drives indoor medical visits and telehealth
- **School Calendar**: Back-to-school (August) and winter break (December) drive family health visits
- **Work Calendar**: Q1 bonus period and year-end planning affect health spending decisions
- **Holidays**: Stress, family time, and schedule changes affect appointment attendance
