# Retail Seasonal Marketing Calendar

This calendar guides campaign timing, audience strategies, and KPI expectations across the year for retail and e-commerce businesses.

## January - New Year Resolution Season

**Timing:** Jan 1-31 (Peak: Jan 1-15)

**Audience Type:**
- New Year resolutionists (fitness, self-care, home improvement)
- Budget-conscious post-holiday shoppers
- Clearance & end-of-season buyers

**Campaign Strategies:**
- New year, new you messaging
- Bundle deals (health & wellness packages)
- Clearance sales for holiday inventory
- Gift card redemption incentives

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- AOV (typically lower due to clearance)
- First-time buyer conversion rate
- Email engagement rate (high open rates for resolutions)
- Cart abandonment rate

**Audience Trigger:** Purchase category interest in fitness, home, wellness; clearance price sensitivity.

## Cross-Season Patterns & Insights

### Year-Round Repeat Buyers
- Loyalty program members
- Subscription box customers
- Seasonal repeat purchasers (same category year-over-year)

**Strategy:** Win these customers early with pre-season offers, loyalty bonuses, and exclusive previews.

### Seasonal Lapsed Customers
- Customers who buy during one season but not others
- Holiday gift-givers who don't self-purchase
- Category-specific seasonal shoppers

**Strategy:** Reactivation campaigns 3-4 weeks into each season; cross-seasonal interest discovery.

### AOV Progression by Season
- **Highest:** December (holidays), November (BFCM), May (Mother's Day), February (Valentine's)
- **Lowest:** January (clearance), Summer (clearance), Back-to-School (price-sensitive)

### Return Rate Patterns
- **Highest:** January (fit/regret post-holiday), Summer (seasonal sizing), post-BFCM
- **Lowest:** Holiday season (gifted items have emotional value), Mother's/Father's Day

### Inventory Planning
- Build inventory: June-July (holiday), August-September (holiday)
- Clear inventory: January, July, early September
- Stock special items: 6-8 weeks before each holiday

## Audience Segmentation Framework by Season

### Always-Active Segments
- High-value customers (CLTV > 90th percentile)
- VIP loyalty members
- Repeat purchasers (3+ purchases)

### Seasonal Segments
- Holiday gift-givers (purchase Dec-Jan for gifts)
- Seasonal fashion buyers (Oct-Nov, Mar-May)
- Occasion-specific buyers (Valentine's, Mother's Day, etc.)
- Sale-driven shoppers (activate during BFCM, clearance)

### Reactivation Opportunities
- Lapsed customers (60-180 days since purchase): Seasonal re-engagement
- One-time buyers: Seasonal trigger for second purchase
- Category dormancy: Cross-seasonal interest campaigns
