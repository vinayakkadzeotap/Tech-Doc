# Telecom Seasonal Marketing Calendar

Strategic calendar for mobile carriers and ISP marketing. Covers device launch cycles, contract renewal windows, network expansion, travel seasonality, and subscriber acquisition moments.

## Q1 - January to March

### January - New Year Contract Resets & Device Launches

**Timing:** Jan 1-31 (Peak: Jan 1-15 for New Year, mid-late Jan for device announcements)

**Business Drivers:**
- New Year resolution budget (gym contracts bundled with data)
- Holiday gift device activations continuing
- Consumer Electronics Show (CES) announcements
- New device pre-orders starting

**Campaign Opportunities:**
- New subscriber onboarding campaigns
- Plan optimization for New Year spenders
- Broadband promotion (home renovation season prep)
- Device launch promotions (CES reveals)
- Contract renewal campaigns (holiday device installers)

**Subscriber Behavior:**
- Returning holiday season over-spenders (churn risk)
- New devices from holiday gifts (stable, low early churn)
- Rural winter season (indoor activities, data usage up)

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- New subscriber SAC and Day 1 churn
- Holiday device cohort stability
- Broadband bundle attach rate
- Device pre-order conversion

---

### February - Device Launch Preparation & Regional Events

**Timing:** Feb 1-28 (Peak: Second half for major device reveals)

**Business Drivers:**
- Major Android OEM announcements (Samsung Galaxy S series typically Feb)
- Valentine's Day consumer spending
- Regional carrier promotional cycles
- Winter season peak for broadband

**Campaign Opportunities:**
- Device trade-in campaigns (trade old for new)
- Premium device upgrade promotions
- Valentine's couple plan promotions (shared data, family plans)
- Broadband speed upgrade campaigns (streaming, video calls)

**Subscriber Behavior:**
- Peak contract renewal window (post-holiday)
- Heavy 4K video consumption (winter indoor behavior)
- Business user data spike (back to office post-holidays)

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- Device upgrade rate
- Trade-in volume
- Couple/family plan conversions
- Broadband speed tier upgrades

---

### March - St. Patrick's Day & Spring Network Launches

**Campaign Opportunities:**

**Subscriber Behavior:**

- Family trip planning (data usage increase, family plans)

**Key Metrics to Track:**

- Roaming plan uptake
- Prepaid reactivation rate
- Family plan conversions

- Upgrade incentive acceptance
- Holiday gift plan adoption

### November - Black Friday, Thanksgiving, Cyber Monday

**Timing:** Nov 1-30 (Peak: Black Friday & Cyber Monday ~Nov 29-Dec 2)

**Business Drivers:**
- Black Friday/Cyber Monday mega sales (device bundles, plan deals)

**Subscriber Behavior:**
- Peak device purchase (BFCM drives annual device sales)
- Family holiday planning (shared data, family plans)
- Cord-cutting trend (streaming over traditional TV)
- Gift giving (subscriptions as gifts)

**Key Metrics to Track:**
- BFCM device sales volume (annual peak)
- Bundle attachment rate (device + plan + extras)
- Streaming service adoption via bundling
- Family plan growth
- Broadband bundling rate

**Key Metrics to Track:**

## Travel Seasonality Patterns

### Peak Roaming Seasons
- **Summer (June-Aug):** +50-100% roaming revenue (international vacation)
- **Winter holidays (Dec):** +30-50% roaming revenue (family visits, holidays)
- **Easter/Spring break (Apr):** +20-40% roaming revenue
- **Thanksgiving (Nov, US):** +20-30% roaming revenue

### Regional Travel Patterns
- **Northern hemisphere winter (Dec-Feb):** Tropical/warm destinations (skiing, holidays)
- **Southern hemisphere summer (Dec-Feb):** Australia, New Zealand peak
- **European summer (Jul-Aug):** Intra-Europe travel peaks
- **Asian Lunar New Year (Jan-Feb):** Regional travel surge

### Business Travel Peaks
- **Q1-Q2:** Conference season (CES, conferences)
- **Spring/Fall:** Trade shows, industry events
- **Q4:** Year-end business travel, holiday visits

## Device Upgrade Cycle Calendar

### Flagship Launch Timeline
- **Sep:** iPhone launch (Apple)
- **Feb:** Samsung Galaxy S (flagship)
- **Aug:** Samsung Galaxy Note (phablet)
- **Ongoing:** Google Pixel, OnePlus, mid-tier OEM launches

### Upgrade Eligibility Tiers
- **Immediate (months 18-24):** Eligible for full upgrade discount
- **Moderate (months 12-18):** Possible early upgrade (penalty)
- **Future (months 0-12):** Contract locked, no incentive to upgrade

### Trade-In Program Peaks
- Post-launch (existing users upgrading): +2-3 weeks post-launch
- Contract renewal: Upgrade incentives bundled
- Seasonal promotions: BFCM, holiday bundles

---

## Network Expansion Calendar

### 5G Rollout Timeline (Typical)
- **Phase 1 (announced):** Hype campaigns, device pre-order bundling
- **Phase 2 (soft launch):** Early access for loyal customers, high-value segments
- **Phase 3 (general availability):** Market-wide campaigns, adoption push
- **Phase 4 (saturation):** Focus on speed superiority, device bundling

### Fiber/Broadband Expansion
- **Q1-Q2:** Rural expansion announcements
- **Q3-Q4:** Suburban deployment peaks (pre-holiday for home installers)
- **Ongoing:** Urban infill (unserved areas in metro regions)

### Spectrum Auction Impact
- **Post-auction (varies):** Network investment announcements
- **18-24 months post:** New spectrum live (5G capacity, 4G enhancements)
