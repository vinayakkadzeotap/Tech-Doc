## 🏢 What is Zeotap?

Zeotap is a **Customer Data Platform (CDP)** that helps enterprises unify, understand, and activate their customer data. Founded in Germany, Zeotap is one of the leading European-born CDPs, built from the ground up with privacy and compliance at its core.

<Callout type="tip" title="The One-Liner">
Zeotap helps brands turn fragmented customer data into unified, actionable profiles — so they can deliver the right experience to the right person at the right time.
</Callout>

### Our Mission

To empower enterprises to understand and engage their customers through ethical, privacy-first data unification and activation.

### What Problem Do We Solve?

Modern enterprises face a fundamental data challenge:

- **Data is fragmented** — Customer interactions happen across websites, mobile apps, email, stores, call centers, and more. Each creates a separate data silo.
- **Identity is broken** — The same person might be `user_123` on web, `device_abc` on mobile, and `email@company.com` in CRM. Without unification, you can't see the full picture.
- **Privacy is complex** — GDPR, CCPA, and evolving regulations make it risky to handle customer data without proper infrastructure.
- **Activation is disconnected** — Marketing, sales, and service teams use different tools with different data, leading to inconsistent customer experiences.

**Zeotap solves all of this** by providing a unified platform that:

1. **Collects** data from every touchpoint
2. **Unifies** identities into a single profile per person (the UCID)
3. **Activates** those profiles to 100+ marketing and analytics destinations

### The Three Pillars

Zeotap's platform is built around three core capabilities:

<ArchDiagram
  title="Zeotap Platform Architecture"
  steps={[
    { label: "Collect", detail: "SDKs, APIs, file imports, real-time streams", icon: "📡" },
    { label: "Unify", detail: "Identity resolution, profile store, AI enrichment", icon: "🔗" },
    { label: "Activate", detail: "Audiences, journeys, 100+ destinations", icon: "🚀" }
  ]}
/>

**1. Collect** — We ingest data from everywhere: web SDKs, mobile SDKs, server-to-server APIs, batch file imports, and real-time event streams. Every customer interaction flows into the platform.

**2. Unify** — Our identity resolution engine links all those fragmented identifiers into a single **Unified Customer Identity (UCID)**. The profile store maintains a complete, up-to-date view of each customer with ACID-compliant transactions.

**3. Activate** — Once you have unified profiles, you can build audiences (segments), create customer journeys, and push data to 100+ destinations including Google Ads, Meta, Salesforce, email platforms, and more.
