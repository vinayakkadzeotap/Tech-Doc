## 🏢 What is Zeotap?

Zeotap is a **Customer Data Platform (CDP)** that helps enterprises unify, understand, and activate their customer data. Founded in Berlin, Germany, Zeotap is one of the leading European-born CDPs, built from the ground up with privacy and compliance at its core. The platform is certified **ISO 27018** (cloud privacy), **ISO 27701** (privacy information management), and **SOC 2 Type II** compliant.

<Callout type="tip" title="The One-Liner">
Zeotap helps brands turn fragmented customer data into unified, actionable profiles — so they can deliver the right experience to the right person at the right time.
</Callout>

### Our Mission

To empower enterprises to understand and engage their customers through ethical, privacy-first data unification and activation.

### What Problem Do We Solve?

Modern enterprises face a fundamental data challenge:

- **Data is fragmented** — Customer interactions happen across websites, mobile apps, email, stores, call centers, and more. Each creates a separate data silo.
- **Identity is broken** — The same person might be `user_123` on web, `device_abc` on mobile, and `email@company.com` in CRM. Without unification, you can't see the full picture.
- **Privacy is complex** — GDPR, CCPA, and evolving regulations make it risky to handle customer data without proper infrastructure.
- **Activation is disconnected** — Marketing, sales, and service teams use different tools with different data, leading to inconsistent customer experiences.

**Zeotap solves all of this** by providing a unified platform that:

1. **Collects** data from every touchpoint
2. **Unifies** identities into a single profile per person (the UCID)
3. **Enriches** profiles with **Zeotap Fuel** (third-party data enrichment with NCE data) and **Calculated Attributes**
4. **Activates** those profiles to **150+ integrations** across marketing and analytics destinations

Zeotap's platform is organized into six core product modules:

**Integrate** — Ingest data from everywhere: web SDKs, mobile SDKs, server-to-server APIs, batch imports via the **Integr8** builder, and 150+ pre-built connectors. Push data to advertising, CRM, email, and analytics destinations.

**Unify** — Our **ID+** identity solution (powered by ~50 billion deterministic IDs) links fragmented identifiers into a single **UCID**. The **Data Catalogue** manages schema, **Calculated Attributes** derive new fields from raw data, and the Profile Store maintains ACID-compliant customer views.

**Segment** — Build audiences using the visual rule builder or **Ada** (Zeotap's AI copilot) for natural language segmentation. Access **2,500+ Champion Segments** as pre-built templates.

**Orchestrate** — Create multi-step customer journeys with triggers, conditions, A/B splits, and cross-channel actions.

**Dashboard** — Monitor platform health, usage analytics, and consumption metrics.

**Protect** — Manage consent (TCF 2.2, GDPR), Customer 360 views, and data lifecycle policies.
