## 🎯 Our Customers

Zeotap serves **enterprise brands** — large companies with complex data ecosystems that need to unify customer data across many touchpoints. Our customers typically process millions to billions of customer profiles and care deeply about data privacy and compliance.

### Key Verticals

<Expandable title="🛍️ Retail & E-commerce" defaultOpen={true}>

**Use Cases:**
- **Personalized recommendations** — Show the right products based on unified browsing + purchase history
- **Cart abandonment recovery** — Trigger multi-channel journeys (email, push, retargeting) when users abandon carts
- **Loyalty program optimization** — Unify online and in-store behavior to personalize loyalty rewards
- **Customer lifetime value (CLV)** — Predict which customers will be most valuable and invest accordingly

**Why They Choose Zeotap:**
Retailers have data in POS systems, e-commerce platforms, mobile apps, loyalty programs, and marketing tools. Zeotap unifies all of it into one profile per shopper, enabling personalization at scale.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="🏦 Financial Services">

**Use Cases:**
- **Customer 360** — Complete view of each client across banking, insurance, and investment products
- **Cross-sell and upsell** — Identify customers ready for additional products based on life events and behavior
- **Regulatory compliance** — GDPR-compliant data handling with full consent management and audit trails
- **Churn prevention** — Detect at-risk customers and trigger retention campaigns

**Why They Choose Zeotap:**
Financial services companies face strict regulations. Zeotap's EU-first architecture and privacy-by-design approach gives them confidence in compliance.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="📱 Telecommunications">

**Use Cases:**
- **Churn prediction** — ML models identify subscribers likely to leave and trigger retention offers
- **Network personalization** — Tailor product recommendations based on usage patterns
- **Upsell to premium plans** — Identify customers using more data/minutes than their plan allows
- **Family/household unification** — Link individual subscribers to household accounts

**Why They Choose Zeotap:**
Telcos have massive customer bases (millions of subscribers) with data in billing systems, network logs, app usage, and retail stores. Scale matters — Zeotap handles it.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="📺 Media & Entertainment">

**Use Cases:**
- **Content personalization** — Recommend content based on cross-platform viewing behavior
- **Audience monetization** — Build and sell premium advertising audiences to advertisers
- **Subscriber acquisition** — Target lookalike audiences based on existing subscriber profiles
- **Ad frequency capping** — Prevent showing the same ad too many times across channels

**Why They Choose Zeotap:**
Media companies need to monetize their first-party data as cookies disappear. Zeotap helps them build rich audience segments from their own data.

</Expandable>

<Expandable title="🚗 Automotive">

**Use Cases:**
- **Lead scoring** — Identify high-intent buyers from website configurator usage, dealer visits, test drives
- **Lifecycle marketing** — Engage customers from research phase through purchase to service
- **Dealer engagement** — Share relevant customer insights with dealer networks
- **Connected car data** — Integrate vehicle telemetry with customer profiles

**Why They Choose Zeotap:**
Automotive purchase journeys are long (3-6 months) and cross many touchpoints. Unifying this journey is critical for effective marketing.

</Expandable>
